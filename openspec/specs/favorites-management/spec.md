## Requirements

### Requirement: CRUD Operations
The system MUST support creating, reading, and deleting favorite strategies.

#### Scenario: Save New Favorite
Given I am on the "Optimization Results" page and I see a profitable result, When I click "Save" and enter the name "Super BTC Strat", Then the strategy configuration AND its results metrics are saved to the persistent store.

#### Scenario: List Favorites
Given I have saved multiple strategies, When I navigate to the "Saved Strategies" page, Then I see a list of all saved strategies with Name, Symbol, and key metrics (Return, Sharpe).

#### Scenario: Delete Favorite
Given I am on the "Saved Strategies" page, When I click "Delete" on a strategy, Then it is removed from the list permanently.

### Requirement: Governed Sequential BTC Favorite Winners
The system SHALL only count a BTC/USDT 1D Long Favorite generated by a sequential hard-mode execution as a winner when it is new versus the T0 snapshot, saved after T0, non-duplicated, non-dominated by current revalidated Favorites, and sequentially superior to every winner already saved in the same execution.

#### Scenario: First winner is saved after T0
- **WHEN** the execution saves `WINNER_1`
- **THEN** the saved Favorite SHALL have an id absent from T0
- **AND** `created_at` SHALL be later than the T0 timestamp
- **AND** `strategy_name` and normalized parameters SHALL not duplicate a T0 Favorite

#### Scenario: Later winner must improve the chain
- **WHEN** the execution evaluates `WINNER_N` after at least one prior winner was saved
- **THEN** the candidate SHALL be compared against every prior winner
- **AND** the candidate SHALL not have lower return than any prior winner
- **AND** the candidate SHALL not have higher max drawdown than any prior winner
- **AND** the candidate SHALL materially improve return, max drawdown, Sharpe, or profit factor according to the issue thresholds before it is saved

#### Scenario: Saved winner is verified through readback
- **WHEN** a winner Favorite is saved
- **THEN** the execution SHALL re-read the Favorite list, individual Favorite, and trades endpoint or equivalent
- **AND** the readback SHALL show updated metrics, deep execution evidence, correct public name, and no generic fallback before the winner is counted
